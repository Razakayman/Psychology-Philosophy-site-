Philocology

Philosophy is defined as "the rational investigation of the truths and principles of being, knowledge, or conduct."
Psychology on the other hand is defined as "the sum or characteristics of the mental states and processes of a person or class of persons, or of the mental states and processes involved in a field of activity."

While both fields are different, they are also quite alike. While Philosophy looks that the truths and the ideals of mankind, psychology looks at the mental state and individual as a whole. These two come together to tell us a lot about ourselves and the lives we choose to live.

This site will be looking at the 4 most important philosphers and psychologists in my opinion. There are plenty of other important individuals in both fields so I limited it to 4 that have changed the game for Philosophy and Psychology respectively.
